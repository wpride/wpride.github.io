Biography

I'm a software engineer living in Boston, Massachusetts. I work primarily in improving healthcare in the developing world; however, I'm frequently working on two or three side projects at any given time. Hence, this website to catalogue my work.

Professionally I work for Dimagi Inc. on the core CommCare product with particular focus on the mobile side in Android and J2ME development. CommCare is a highly configurable and accessible tool for case management, data analysis, and data management with a particular focus on the developing world.

My primary interests outside coding are baseball, politics, and data analysis and algorithms; as such, many of my side projects are data tools developed around these areas. You can find them in my GitHub and BitBucket accounts along with coursework from related classes.
